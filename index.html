How to Build a Jewlery Collection

A guide to creating your dream jewlery ensemble from a simple look to an elaborate one!

Step 1: Basics
Step 2: Stacking
Step 3: Gemstones, Colors, Chunky, Metal Mixing


Step 1: Basics

First, you want to figure out if you prefer gold or silver jewlery. After this, target finding pieces that are simple and low key. These are things like simple chain necklaces or single charm ones, hoops, plain rings and bands and simple chain or woven bracelets. For a lot of people, this is the extent of complexity they want their jewlery to be which is totally fine. Everyone is different and a look composed of these items can look super pretty and good for everyday!

Step 2: Stacking

Many people like to put several necklaces together and mix and match all of their jewlery. The best way to do this is to balance between lenghts and complexity of the pieces. You want a good balance of the more plain jewlery like a chain necklace with the more chunky and attention grabbing pieces.

Step 3: Gemstones, Colors, Chunky, Metal Mixing

If you want to create a more bold look, you can utilize mixing metals, wearing jewlery with gems or beads of different colors and chunkier jewlery. This look can also include a lot of stacking. Even if you want a simple everday look, having some necklaces or earrings to add on can dress up an outfit or be an outfit change for going out at night.



Overall, jewlery is a great way to express yourself, complete an outfit and feel confident!
